📦 Terraform Modules

What are Terraform Modules?

Terraform Modules are reusable units of infrastructure-as-code. They help you organize and reuse configurations by grouping related resources together.

Use Cases:

  • Encapsulate reusable infrastructure patterns
  • Standardize deployments across environments
  • Promote best practices and DRY (Don’t Repeat Yourself) principles

Common Module Types:

  • vpc – Define networking and subnets
  • ec2_instance – Launch reusable EC2 modules
  • s3_bucket – Create compliant S3 buckets
  • iam_roles – Manage standardized IAM roles
